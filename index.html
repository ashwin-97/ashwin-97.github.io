SCARA Robotic arm

Though, the technology is aimed at increasing efficiency and accuracy by achieving optimization in the traditional manufacturing methods, its most major drawback is the high cost of maintenance and installation. SCARA robotic arm is our low cost, effective alternative to the CNC Drilling machine.

Mobile application to detect Maxilofacial diseases

Maxillofacial surgery is a facial-dental procedure that particularises in the treatment of face, mouth, and jaws. Despite being one of the most rapidly expanding medical fields, it is still unknown to most of the general public . Our tool detects the aforementioned diseases by using computer vision and maxillofacial techniques. Currently we are in progress to extend this project to help the surgeons and patients visualise change in facial features after the surgery.

  • Customer Industry: Healthcare

Autonomous Solar Bot

Over history, agriculture has evolved from a manual occupation to a highly industrialized business, utilizing a wide variety of tools and machines. Researchers are now looking towards the realization of autonomous agricultural vehicles. The potential benefits of automated agricultural vehicles include increased productivity, increased application accuracy, and enhanced operation safety. Additionally, the rapid advancement in electronics, computers, and computing technologies has inspired renewed interest in the development of vehicle guidance systems. We have designed an autonomous solar bot to assist small to mid-range farmers in the cumbersome farming process. India experiences a high mortality rate in the agricultural sector due to high amounts of debt. This bot aims to automate the process and automate various aspects of the farming process. Current Technology assisted farming techniques, exist, but at very high costs which are inaccessible to the general Indian Farmer. An elaborate electromechanical design with the Application of IoT through mobile control is implemented in the bot with a novel machine learning algorithm that suggests the best crop to be used in the sample soil. A dual axis tracker is also incorporated to actively control the solar panel to maximise solar energy percentage. This bot is currently in the testing phase with possible plans for mass production.

  • Date: In Progress
  • Category: Mechanical Design and Robotics
  • Customer Industry: Agriculture
